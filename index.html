Why This Story Exists

A life spent
helping others grow.

Life often demands that we care for everyone else's growth first — the classroom, the family, the community — while the quiet work of tending our own mind, body, and spirit waits its turn.

"This book is a reminder that becoming a good mother, a strong leader, and a whole person can happen in the same lifetime — the same day, even."

Written by an educator who has spent over 25 years shaping young lives, Beneath the Lotus is a personal reflection on what it means to nurture — others, and yourself — without losing either.

The Person Behind the Story

Palinyakone Panyathip

Educator · Leader · Mother · Dreamer

With over 25 years of experience in international education, Palinyakone Panyathip is CEO of Panyathip British International School and Executive Vice President — International Development at St Hugh's College Vientiane.

A Chevening Scholar and passionate advocate for wellbeing, she believes true education nurtures not only the mind, but also the heart and spirit. Beneath the Lotus is a reflection of her own journey — lessons from life, purpose, and the belief that we all have the power to grow, heal, and bloom.

"I didn't write this to be admired. I wrote it because every woman deserves permission to bloom on her own time."
